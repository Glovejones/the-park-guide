A note from the editor

We built Wildside because we were tired of driving twenty minutes to a park that turned out to be a patch of dead grass and a broken swing.

California has 12,619 parks. Most of them are extraordinary. A handful are genuinely life-changing — the kind where your kid asks to go back before you've even left the car park. The problem was never the parks. It was finding them.

So we scored every one. Neighborhood safety, school quality, walkability, how close you are to a decent coffee after the chaos. We talked to parents, we ran the Census numbers, we drove the routes. Then we wrote it up the way we wished someone had written it for us.

Wildside is free. It always will be. The only thing we ask is that you get outside.
